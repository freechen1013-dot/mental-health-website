About Us

We are students who wanted to take the chance of our school's mental health project to inform people about mental health maintenance. This website is an extension of our project, but we decided to take a further step for people to engage in the education of mental health wellness.

As a student and a singer-songwriter, I often use music as a way to encourage people and improve their mental wellness. My first music album contains songs like Self-search and Beautiful that encourages my listeners to find their true self and appreciate the beauty of our world. By making this website, I want to influence more people to stay positive and healthy in their lives!
